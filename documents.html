This literature review explores the key factors that influence the successful implementation of e-learning, including technological infrastructure, learner engagement, and instructor preparedness.
It discusses the challenges and benefits of e-learning, particularly in diverse cultural and socioeconomic contexts, and highlights the effectiveness of blended learning as a successful approach. The review concludes by emphasizing the need for continued research to improve access and adaptability across different educational levels and regions. .
